Beyond All Reason: Beginner Cheat Sheet

Quick reference for essential tips during intense gameplay. Glance and act fast!

Game Settings
Core Settings
  • Enable Grid Optimized Hotkeys (Controls) for intuitive bindings. Customize via Discord.
  • Activate Set Target as Default for right-click targeting (e.g., enemy Commander).
  • Keep Player List active for rank & skill score visibility.
Interface & Customization
  • Use Build Grid Menu with builders (e.g., Commander) for easy placement.
  • Enable "Always Show Prices", "Group Icon", "Radar Icon" for key info.
  • Adjust Icon Scale/Distance for unit visibility (e.g., Constructors).
  • Explore Widgets (DPS counts, defense ranges) for playstyle customization.
  • Set Accessibility team colors (one per team or unique per player).
Hotkeys & Commands
Commander Shortcuts
  • Ctrl+C: Find/select Commander anywhere on map.
  • Ctrl+B: Cycle idle buildings/workers.
  • F3: Jump to ping/alert location.
Basic Commands
  • Area Commands: Ctrl+Z or double-tap for area tasks (build Metal Extractors, repair, attack).
  • Reclaim: Grab metal from resources/wrecks; critical early (self-destruct Commander = 2000 metal).
  • Repeat (T Key): Repeat builds (Grunts) or moves; Shift+Q for patrol under fire.
  • Queue: Hold Alt for single unit (Constructor) without repeat; command mid-build (Advanced Fusion Reactor).
Unit & Navigation
  • Select/Split: Q for all of type (Grunts); Ctrl+Q splits; Ctrl+Z or double-click for all on map/in view.
  • Fight vs. Move: Fight for backline (stop to attack); Move for frontline (fire while advancing).
  • Draw/Ping: Tilde+Q to draw; Tilde+Middle Click for pings; Tilde+Right Click to delete.
  • Camera: Ctrl+Scroll (up/down), Alt (fast); Ctrl+T (overview); Ctrl+F7 (hide UI); Alt+edge or middle mouse for perspective.
  • Idle Units: Ctrl+Tap for idle Constructor; Tab for Commander.
  • Auto Groups: Alt+Number (Grunts on 1); persists; Ctrl+Tilde to remove.
Commander Abilities
Combat & Stealth
  • D-Gun (Hotkey D): 500 energy, one-hit kill, short range; avoid experimental units.
  • Cloak (Hotkey K): Near-invisible; low energy still, high moving; detectable by radar/close enemies.
  • Self-Destruct (Ctrl+D): Detonate for surprise damage/metal denial; check radius with Space+X.
Utility
  • Build Power: Highest in game; assist critical projects for speed.
  • Radar Range: Green dotted line beyond sight; attack fog of war (inaccurate without support).
Team Game Basics
Setup & Positioning
  • Starting Spot: Pick area with 3+ Metal Extractors; edge for breakthroughs, backline for eco (units for newbies).
  • Pre-Game Builds: Queue initial builds (e.g., Metal Extractors) before start.
Resources & Tech
  • Resource Share: Drag UI sliders for metal/energy; limit early storage (1-2 max).
  • Unit Share: Double-click share icon for units (T2 Constructors); avoid Commander mishaps.
  • T2 Timing: Delay T2 (2900 metal); wait ~10 min or reclaim; not for beginners.
  • T1 Focus: Add T1 facilities (Bot Lab after Vehicle Lab) over early T2.
Army Commands & Tactics
Positioning & Movement
  • Formation (Ctrl+Click): Move group at slowest unit speed for coordination.
  • Line Spread (Hold-Click): Drag line to evenly spread army; ideal for cliffs/surrounding.
Combat Tactics
  • Flanking Bonus: Attack from multiple sides for increased damage output.
  • Laser Damage: 50% at max range, 100% close; adjust positioning.
  • Area Actions: Drag-click for repair/attack/reclaim zone.
Unit & Game Details
Unit Specifics
  • Rank Benefits: Higher rank = faster reload, more health; prioritize veterans.
  • Terrain: Bots best on rough terrain; Amphibious Vehicles handle uneven; Hovering Vehicles immune to submarine torpedoes.
  • Icon Indicators: Corners show attack type (air/land/underwater); check for strategy.
Game Customization
  • Spawn Zones: Adjust location/number/size when setting up games.
Multiplayer & Bonus Tips
Multiplayer Setup
  • Pre-Test: Load 1 AI game to avoid multiplayer crashes/load delays.
  • Finding Games: Check battle list/Discord; green=open, yellow=started, blue=empty/custom.
  • Join Lobbies: Pick noob lobbies (rank <20); queue with "!notify" for slot alerts.
Efficiency Tips
  • Build Grid: Shift+Alt for box builds (Wind Turbines); Space splits tasks; Z/X spreads vs. explosions.
  • Metal View (F7): Toggle for untaken Metal Extractors; keep radar for map light.
  • Explosion Safety: Space Wind Turbines (1 space via Z/X) vs. chain reactions.
  • Resources: Check BAR online guides/Discord for more tips.
